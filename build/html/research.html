Working Groups

DTL main

Our working group sessions are conducted throughout the academic year, bringing together Lab members for discussions on readings and the theoretical dimensions of computational practice. Past syllabi are available on request. The working group is currently run by Tyler Shoemaker.

LOLM

The Language of Language Machines (LOLM) is a working group to produce a critical vocabulary for understanding contemporary (post-2015) natural language processing (NLP) literature. The working group is currently run by Lachlan Kermode and Ryan Healey.
